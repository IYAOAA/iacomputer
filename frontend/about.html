About I A Computer Academy

A community-driven computer training and digital service centre built to empower people through technology.

Who We Are

I A Computer Academy is a small but growing computer training and digital service centre dedicated to helping individuals, students, and small businesses gain practical computer skills and access essential digital services. We focus on simplicity, patience, and real-world learning.

Our Focus

Education

Structured computer training and digital skills development.

Digital Services

Practical solutions for individuals, students, and businesses.

Technology Growth

Building a platform for future digital tools and systems.

Our Mission

To provide affordable, accessible, and practical computer education and digital services that empower people with skills for education, employment, and everyday life.

Our Vision

To become a trusted community technology centre where anyone can learn, grow, and confidently use digital tools to improve their future.
