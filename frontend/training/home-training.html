Home Training Programme

Personalized computer training delivered to your location within Suleja, Abuja, and surrounding areas.

Overview

Our Home Training Programme is designed for individuals and small groups who prefer learning in a more flexible and personalized environment.

Training sessions are conducted at your location, with a focus on practical skills and step-by-step guidance.

How It Works

1. Request

Contact us and specify the course you are interested in.

2. Schedule

We agree on a suitable time and training plan.

3. Training

Sessions are delivered at your location.

Available Courses

Computer Basics

Learn how to use a computer, internet, and email.

Microsoft Office

Word, Excel, and PowerPoint for productivity.

Graphic Design

Create designs using CorelDRAW.

Website Design (Intro)

Learn the basics of building websites.

Who This Is For

  • Beginners who prefer one-on-one learning
  • Students preparing for exams or projects
  • Busy individuals needing flexible schedules
  • Small groups or families

Areas Covered

Our home training services are available in:

  • Abuja (selected areas)
